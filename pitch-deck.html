Pitch Deck 2024

SAMARPAN

Where classrooms become arenas and learning becomes legendary.

Samarpan is a real-time multiplayer quiz and tournament platform that turns any classroom into a competitive, gamified learning arena — powered by AI and an Elo-style rating system.

Built for college professors, students, coaching institutes, and event organizers across India.
01 — Problem Statement

The Classroom is Broken

  • Zero engagement: Google Forms and paper tests are passive, one-way, and boring — students check out within minutes.
  • No real-time feedback: Teachers have no visibility into how many students understood a concept until the test is graded — days later.
  • Kahoot doesn't fit India: Existing tools are built for Western K-12 classrooms. They lack competitive depth, AI generation, and the rating/ranking culture that Indian students thrive on.
  • No competitive layer: There is no platform that combines live quizzes with a persistent rating system — students have no motivation to improve beyond a single session.
  • Cheating is rampant: Online quizzes on Google Forms and Quizizz are trivially easy to cheat — students switch tabs, Google answers, and share screens. Teachers have no way to detect or prevent it.
"Every day, 40 million Indian college students sit through lectures with zero interactive assessment — teachers have no idea who is actually learning, and no way to stop students from cheating on online tests."
02 — Solution

Real-Time. AI-Powered. Competitive.

  • Live multiplayer quizzes: Host creates a session, shares a 6-digit PIN — students join instantly. Questions broadcast simultaneously with a synced countdown timer.
  • AI quiz generation: Teachers type a topic or upload a PDF — Samarpan generates a full quiz in seconds using Groq LLM. No prep time needed.
  • Elo-style rating system: Every student earns a persistent rating across Rapid, Blitz, and Casual modes — just like chess.com. Learning becomes a ranked sport.
  • Host control panel: Real-time answer distribution, kick/ban moderation, result reveal on demand — the teacher is always in control.
Core value proposition: Samarpan is the only platform that combines live classroom quizzing, AI content generation, and a persistent competitive rating system — built specifically for the Indian education market.
Why now? Post-COVID, Indian colleges are actively seeking digital engagement tools. LLM APIs are now cheap enough to power real-time AI generation at scale. The EdTech gamification wave is just beginning in India's Tier 2 and Tier 3 colleges.
03 — Product Overview / How It Works

Simple to Host. Addictive to Play.

👨‍🏫 Teacher / Host Flow

  • Log in → Create quiz manually or generate via AI in 30 seconds
  • Click "Host Quiz" → Get a unique 6-digit PIN
  • Watch students join live on the lobby screen
  • Start the game → Questions auto-broadcast with synced timer
  • See live answer distribution per option in real-time
  • End quiz → Reveal results and leaderboard on your command

🎮 Student / Player Flow

  • Open Samarpan → Enter PIN shared by teacher
  • Enter name → Join the live lobby instantly
  • Answer questions before the timer runs out
  • Change answer anytime before timer ends
  • See live leaderboard after each question
  • Earn rating points — climb the global leaderboard

Three Ways to Monetize

Model 01

Freemium SaaS

Free: up to 30 players/session, 5 AI quizzes/month

Pro: ₹299/teacher/month — unlimited players, unlimited AI, PDF upload, advanced analytics

Model 02

Institutional License

Annual contract per college or coaching institute

₹49,999/year per institution — unlimited teachers, custom branding, admin dashboard, priority support

Model 03

Tournament Monetization

Inter-college tournaments with entry fees

₹99–₹499/student entry — sponsored leaderboards, prize pools, branded events for coaching institutes

  • Year 1 focus: Institutional licensing — one contract pays more than 100 individual Pro subscriptions.

9–18 Months
Scale
