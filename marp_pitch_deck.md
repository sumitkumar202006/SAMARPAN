## The Solution: Project Samarpan
**A Strictly Synchronized Multiplayer Engine**

- **Strict Synchronization:** Robust real-time WebSockets engine ensures zero desync.
- **Dynamic Lobbies:** Real-time slots, team/solo competition, live avatars.
- **Anti-Cheat & Fairness:** "No-answer-reveal" and focus-mode.
- **Premium Analytics:** Actionable insights, shareable cards, challenge invites.
